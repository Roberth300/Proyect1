Solar energy consulting

Solar energy consulting is based on the study of the solar resource in a
certain area, its viability and profitability and the design of the solar
energy generation plant.

Solar energy generation assembly

The assembly of a generation plant is the technical process of assembling
the structure, solar panels, inverters, wiring, transformers, interconnection
with a substation and connection to the electrical grid.
